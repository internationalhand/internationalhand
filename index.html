Our Mission

International Hand Foundation (IHF) is a not-for-profit 501(c)(3) corporation, and our specific purpose is to provide charitable assistance to communities in developing countries that are suffering from extreme poverty. We seek to provide educational opportunities to children in those communities.

IHF is 100% volunteer operated. Every dollar contributed goes directly to the community in need, improving the lives of children and families worldwide. Education is our top priority. When you educate a child you empower them, enable them to build a brighter future and give them the opportunity to succeed.
