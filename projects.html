International Hand Foundation Scholarship Fund

Our first project is the IHF Scholarship Fund. We are raising money to sponsor children and young adults in need so that they may continue their education. In Uganda, the government sponsors schooling for primary education (1-6) under the Universal Primary Education policy. However, the cost of schooling in many developing countries prevents many children from attending school beyond the primary level. Less than 20% of children that finish the 6th grade continue on to secondary level. Our scholarship program allows students who would otherwise drop out of school the opportunity to complete secondary school, or even to attend a local college. The cost of sending a child to school for one year is between $350 for Secondary and $1,300 yr University.

By giving children a chance to attend school we can change their lives, one semester at a time. See our progress, we are changing the lives of these children with your help. See our photos on Flickr, IHF Uganda or watch our video below.
